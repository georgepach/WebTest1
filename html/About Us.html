About Us

What is C'Groovy?

Founded in 2016, C’Groovy is a music streaming platform built for music lovers and creators alike. We are powered by a passionate community of listeners and artists who are always exploring what’s new and exciting in music. From local talents to international hits, our ever-growing library features millions of songs across diverse genres.

C’Groovy empowers artists with smart tools, data-driven recommendations, and seamless publishing access — giving them the stage to grow and connect with global audiences. For listeners, C’Groovy delivers a highly personalized experience, combining intuitive design, curated playlists, and mood-based discovery features.

With users across the world and an evolving digital ecosystem, we are more than just a streaming app — we’re building a community where music connects people.
